The WSU Sales Club develops tomorrow's top sales professionals through hands-on competitions, industry mentorship, and a community of Cougars who compete to win.

What We Do

Compete. Connect.
Grow.

🏆

Sales Competitions

We send teams to regional and national collegiate sales competitions, giving members real-world experience in role-play selling scenarios with industry judges.

🤝

Industry Mentorship

Connect one-on-one with sales leaders from top companies. Get career advice, referrals, and insight into what it really takes to excel in a sales career.

📚

Skill Workshops

Weekly meetings feature workshops on negotiation, cold outreach, CRM tools, and presentation skills — practical training you can use immediately.

💼

Recruiting Pipeline

Our alumni and corporate partners actively recruit from our club. Members get early access to internships and full-time roles at leading sales organizations.

"Sales is the lifeblood of every company. At WSU Sales Club, we make sure Cougar graduates are ready to lead from day one."
